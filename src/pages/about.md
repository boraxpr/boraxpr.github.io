## What I do: Know the WHY behind the code

Driven by a fundamental need to understand how things work under the hood without leaving unknowns on the table. What separates me is my reliance on first principles: I learn directly from raw documentation and architecture rather than relying on boilerplate examples or waiting for spoon-fed tutorials. If a technology exists in my orbit, I am compelled to understand its mechanics. This curiosity allows me to connect the dots across the entire stack, from raw requirements to production reliability.
