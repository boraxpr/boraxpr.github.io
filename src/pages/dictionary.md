*A translation guide from marketing language to reality.*

---

## PRO

**Definition:**  
Nothing fundamentally new. Minor changes packaged so you feel justified paying more.

**Reality:**  
Same product. Slightly nicer defaults.  
Your workflow doesn’t change. Your bill does.

---

## MAX

**Definition:**  
Nothing new. Extremely expensive. Designed to make you feel rich.

**Reality:**  
You paid for status.  
Performance, reliability, and outcomes remain unchanged.

---

## ENTERPRISE

**Definition:**  
The same product, now with contracts, meetings, and a sales rep.

**Reality:**  
You’re paying to outsource responsibility and sleep better.  
The system itself did not improve.

---

## AI-POWERED

**Definition:**  
A thin wrapper around a model call.

**Reality:**  
Latency increased.  
Accuracy unchanged.  
Marketing improved.

---

## NEXT-GEN

**Definition:**  
Current generation with a new name.

**Reality:**  
Version bump.  
Same constraints.  
Same bottlenecks.

---

## TURBO

**Definition:**  
Promised speed improvements.

**Reality:**  
No measurable speedup.  
A safety check was removed, or a timeout was changed.

---

## SMART

**Definition:**  
Heuristic-based behavior with confidence.

**Reality:**  
When it fails, no one knows why.  
When it works, no one knows how.

---

## OPTIMIZED

**Definition:**  
One benchmark improved.

**Reality:**  
The rest of the system stayed the same, or got worse.

---

## SEAMLESS

**Definition:**  
All complexity is hidden.

**Reality:**  
You’ll discover it during an incident.

---

## SCALABLE

**Definition:**  
Works under ideal conditions.

**Reality:**  
Costs grow faster than traffic.

---

## BATCHING

**Definition:**  
Grouping work to reduce overhead.

**Reality:**  
Good for throughput.  
Irrelevant for first-byte latency.

---

## PAGINATION

**Definition:**  
Splitting output into chunks.

**Reality:**  
Input stayed the same.  
Network cost unchanged.

---

## CLOUD-NATIVE

**Definition:**  
Built on managed services.

**Reality:**  
You traded control for convenience.  
Physics still applies.

---

## LIGHTWEIGHT

**Definition:**  
Smaller and simpler.

**Reality:**  
Features were removed.  
You’ll add them back later.

---

## MODULAR

**Definition:**  
Composed of many components.

**Reality:**  
Debugging now requires context switching.

---

## FLEXIBLE

**Definition:**  
Highly configurable.

**Reality:**  
Undefined behavior.  
You are the test suite.

---

## EASY TO USE

**Definition:**  
Beginner-friendly interface.

**Reality:**  
Hard to misuse — until you need to do something real.

---

## PRODUCTION-READY

**Definition:**  
Advertised as safe for real workloads.

**Reality:**  
Someone else is already running it.  
You’re about to learn why they regret it.

---

## FREE

**Definition:**  
No upfront cost.

**Reality:**  
You’ll pay in time, risk, or loss of control.

---

## OPEN-SOURCE

**Definition:**  
Source code is available.

**Reality:**  
Maintenance is your problem now.

---

## STABLE

**Definition:**  
No recent breaking changes.

**Reality:**  
The bugs are just well-known.
